## Compatibility Notes
- NH72 shares NH35 platform — same cases, same hands
- Only unique part = the skeleton dial itself
- Skeleton dials may need adhesive tabs (dial dots) instead of dial feet
- Display/clear caseback recommended to show movement from rear
